John Aniston

He is an American actor, originally from Greece. He was born as Giannis Anastasakis (Greek: Γιάννης Αναστασάκης) in Chania on the island of Crete, although his parents immigrated to the US when he was two years old.

Salacıoğlu

He was a Greek Sufist poet from the late 18th and early 19th centuries. Through his poems, we can know that he has traveled extensively and has spent a few years in Istanbul, most of the time with another renowned Sufi of his time, Haşim Baba.

Joseph Sifakis

He is a computer scientist born in 1946. He studied Electrical Engineering and Computer Science and won the 2007 Turing Prize.

Eleftherios Venizelos

This was one of the most important politicians in modern Greece, serving as Prime Minister of Greece seven times. He studied law at the University of Athens.

Domenikos Theotokopoulos

Known as El Greco, he was a late Renaissance painter who was born in 1541 in Crete. His work consists of large canvases for church altarpieces, numerous devotional paintings and a group of portraits.

Vitsentzos Kornaros

He was a Greek poet. He wrote the epic love poem Erotokritos, a masterpiece of the Greek Renaissance, and perhaps the greatest achievement in modern Greek literature.

Odysseas Elytis

He was a Greek poet, winner of the Nobel Prize for Literature in 1979, considered one of the renovators of Greek poetry in the course of the 20th century.

Nikos Kazantzakis

He was a Greek writer and author of poems and novels. Possibly, he is the most important writer and philosopher of the 20th century and the one who has been translated into the most languages.
